The Project

The world has changed. A medium to transfer data through the air has been developed.
Now we already know of such a concept but our conscience told us to develop something that does not have any side effects.
The first invention with this technology is a human eye lens that constantly records and transmits data to a USB.

The Making

Our group was saddened to see the plight of so many people who couldn't see the magical world around us. We were motivated to build something for the people. And guess, Psyvision has turned out to be the most successful venture till now. We took to the sheets and chalked out a design and a structure for the project.
